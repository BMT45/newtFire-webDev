Fall 2015: Digital Humanities: Coding and Digital Archives

This course prioritized work with TEI XML over historical documents and manuscripts, and concentrated on XSLT and website development to produce effective reading interfaces that address a research question. Course and project sites are now hosted on newtfire.org, web space supported by the Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center.
