XSLT Exercise 2

The assignment

Create an XSLT stylesheet that will transform Bad Hamlet into a hierarchical outline of the titles of acts and scenes in HTML. This isn’t very interesting on its own, of course, but if you were transforming the entire document into HTML for publication on the web, this might serve as the skeleton. It might also stand on its own as a table of contents at the top of such a publication, so that the reader could click on the title of a scene to jump to that location in the file.

If you’re feeling adventurous, you’re welcome to include more information, whether of a publication-oriented sort (e.g., speakers, speeches, stage directions, etc., as if you were publishing the entire play) or as a foray into exploration and analysis (e.g., list of characters who speak in each scene, perhaps with a count of their speeches, length of speeches, etc).
